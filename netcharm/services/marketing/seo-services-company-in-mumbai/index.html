What is our definition of SEO?

‘Search Engine Optimization’ also known as SEO is a marketing procedure of increasing quality traffic to your website through ‘non-paid’ i.e. organic search result. SEO is a terminology that consists of skilful methods, strategies, tactics or techniques used to increase the amount of visitors to your website by securing a high-ranking on the search results pages of a search engine like – Google, Bing, Yahoo and other search engines.

What is On-Page Optimization?

On-Page SEO in simple terms can be defined as a practice of optimizing a web page in a website so that it ranks higher and acquires more relevant traffic through search engines like Google, Bing and Yahoo. On-Page elements like both content and HTML source code of a page can be optimized if you compare to Off-Page SEO which deals with links and other external indicators of traffic.

What is Off-Page Optimization?

Off-Page Optimization can be referred to as measures that need to be or can be taken outside the actual website that helps it to rank higher in search engine rankings. Off-Page SEO focuses on multiple factors, which are not controlled by the coding on your page or by you. Link popularity is one of the best examples of off-page optimization. The aim here is to create a positive reputation for your website by submitting quality content to various other web pages and websites. It helps to create as many as high-quality backlinks (incoming links) possible.
