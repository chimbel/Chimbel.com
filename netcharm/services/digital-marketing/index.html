What is a Digital Marketing Agency?

A Digital Marketing Agency (also known as Digital agency) is an advertising agency that has the ability to adapt to the changing needs of marketing in the ever-growing digital space.
Digital Marketing Agencies bring together a group of creative people like content writers, graphic designers, copywriters, strategists, consultants, developers with modern marketing techniques and the latest technology. They build every digital marketing strategy from the ground- up to deliver final measurable outcomes. In today’s’ world marketing and advertising is primarily digital. A technical digital agency makes sure everything is functional in design right from what type of device is being used, to web development and UX/UI web design to SEO, social media and content marketing – and everything in between. It makes sure the digital efforts bring in business growth along with making a good looking website. With traditional marketing techniques like print, radio, television still upholding marketing relevance, what makes ‘digital agency’ so different?
A Digital Marketing Agency is solely focused on results-based marketing which is measurable and aims to give the best ROI thereby making it much different than a traditional marketing agency. Radio, Tv spots and magazine ads are a thing of the past now. A Digital Marketing Agency is a pit stop for businesses, services and brands that focuses on brand awareness and acquiring new leads with quantifiable results. Digital marketing agencies specialize in a specific category of advertising and marketing. It might be a niche market, a general business size or a specific client base. They hire digital marketing experts for specific marketing needs and gauge more experience rather than trying to be everything to everyone.

Nobody knows the brand better than you and you want someone to communicate and create brand awareness apart from you. It is very much essential to strike a perfect balance between in-house participation and outsourcing your marketing requirements.
Many agencies might openly admit that they have little expertise. There are agencies that offer all the above-mentioned services but aren’t adept to all of them. Look at their website to figure out which services are actually in the agency’s spotlight. An easy way to make out is to check out their homepage where all their core services will be highlighted. What is a full-service digital marketing agency?

The Digital Marketing Agency Services are divided into two marketing categories: Full Service and Niche.
Niche agency solely focuses on a single area of digital marketing. For example, some companies will only focus on developing your website and the other might only focus on Social Media Marketing. While it might help you to cut some budget costs but you as a client will have to coordinate with multiple agencies which in the long term can be a tiresome process to deal with. Focusing on multiple agencies will create a lot of confusion and might end up with multiple perspectives on digital marketing.
A digital marketing agency that manages all the business avenues of a company's online presence is called full-service digital marketing agency. Such agencies focus on one area through multiple marketing departments like web design, web development, SEO, SMM, Social Media Management, Search Engine Marketing, and Social Media Optimization.
The aforementioned departments work as a team to come up with a holistic marketing strategy for a company, determine their KPI, and analyze how each and every department can contribute in the accomplishment of an end goal i.e. conversion.
The UX and design team can help in creating an eye-catchy, user and mobile-friendly design for a website. Website development can be handled by the development team and while this process is underway the SEO, content-writers and the SEM department can focus on their respective goals.
So in a full-service or full-stack digital marketing agency, you can get the best UX and design strategy, best content strategy, best SEO strategy, best SEM strategy, and best Social Media strategy all under one roof.
